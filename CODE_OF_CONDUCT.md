# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

JasWolf is built for companionship, and we want its community to reflect that.
We pledge to make participation a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

In short: **be kind, be constructive, assume good faith.**

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or other unwelcome conduct.

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)**
as its full standard of behavior. Please read it for the complete guidelines,
the enforcement guidelines, and the definitions of unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (the repository, issues,
pull requests, and discussions) and when an individual is representing the
project in public spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainer:

- Open a confidential report via the repository's **Security → Report a
  vulnerability** ([private form](https://github.com/iamvinay5555/jaswolf/security/advisories/new)), or
- Contact the maintainer [@iamvinay5555](https://github.com/iamvinay5555).

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

> Maintainer note: replace the reporting channel above with a dedicated email
> if you prefer a non-GitHub contact.
